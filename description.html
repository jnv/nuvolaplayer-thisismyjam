This Is My Jam is for sharing one song at a time. Pick the one that means the most to you right now and discover which songs truly matter to your friends

Source: Official website

Country Availability

This Is My Jam has no known geographical restrictions, however since the music is actually streamed by third party services, some songs may be blocked in some countries by the underlying service (most notably by YouTube).

Playback Requirements

This Is My Jam does not stream music, the playback is provided by third party services through their embedded players. Therefore the playback requirements of these services apply.

Most of these services support or require a Flash plugin (e.g.YouTube, Official.fm, Hype Machine), some may prefer HTML5 audio (e.g. SoundCloud, Bandcamp, Vimeo).

Known issues

  • Last.fm Support: This Is My Jam supports scrobbling directly from their service, using this feature both in Nuvola and TIMJ may result in duplicate submissions to Last.fm.
  • Incorrect album art: When the profile page is visited, the album art present on the page is used regardfless of the currently playing song.
